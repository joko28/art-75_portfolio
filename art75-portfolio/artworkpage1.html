3D Model, 2020

My final project for Art 75 is to create a 3D model, a room filled with artworks, such as images, 2D, or 3D arts.

The possibilities in life are endless, however people forget the reason why life was created. Animals were born before humans, the galaxy was born before Earth, and the universe was born before anything. I wanted to show the world the endless reasons why nature should be understood more. To show the beauty of the stars, because nowadays no one cares about nature as much as they used to. People are always on their phone, computer, or any type of technology and not being grateful for what nature has given us. The bunny represents peace and love, because bunnies are innocent and adorable. The elephant represents possibilities and adventure, because they are only found in special areas of the planet, you can't see them near unless you go to a zoo. The tree represents life and death, because it is green, it grows, it feeds, but it can also die just like a human. Nature is everything in this world, and everyone takes that for granted, so this garden represents what people have been missing.
